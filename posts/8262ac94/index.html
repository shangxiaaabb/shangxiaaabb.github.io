论文研究目的

探究:基因与细胞类型

Feature gene selection has significant impact on the performance of cell clustering in single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) analysis.

那么需要同时考虑到以下3个因素：

1、relevance | 相关性

2、redundancy | 冗余性

3、complementarity | 互补性

部分研究只侧重研究：基因相关性与细胞类型，本论文探究：maximizing relevance, minimizing redundancy and preserving complementarity

1、Relevance refers to the amount of information a feature can provide for distinguishing classes.

2、Redundancy means that features with similar patterns are redundant to each other for identifying classes.

冗余性理解为：基因（很多）但是并非都和细胞类型之间有关联，有些可能是多余（冗余）的。

3、Complementarity refers to the additional discriminative power when a pair of dependent genes are both selected rather than only one is selected.

通过赛选一对基因而非独立一个，成对的可以提供额外的判别信息。

论文提出方法：$\color{red}{GeneClust}$两种方法

  • 1、GeneClust-ps

leverages a pseudo-semi-supervised algorithm（伪半监督算法） to address the FS problem under the information theory framework

  • 2、GeneClust-fast

relies on fast heuristic（快速启发式） machine learning techniques

比较：法1计算效率低但是更加有效
